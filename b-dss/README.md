# B-DSS: Biomass Decision Support System

A decision support system for biomass availability, logistics, and market analysis developed at Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL).

## Overview

B-DSS provides tools and models to support decision-making around:

- **Biomass Availability** – Estimates of feedstock supply across spatial regions
- **Logistics & Supply Chain** – Transportation, storage, and handling cost modeling
- **Market Analysis** – Price and demand projections for biomass commodities
- **Scenario Analysis** – Comparative evaluation of biomass utilization pathways
